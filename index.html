💰 Economy Management: The Key to Victory

Most beginners lose because they neglect their economy. Here's the optimal build order:

  • First 60 seconds: Train 3 miners immediately
  • Upgrade castle to level 2 before building army
  • Maintain 4-5 miners throughout the game
  • Control central statue for +50% gold income
  • Don't queue units – spend gold as you get it
💡 Pro Tip: In the early game, a single Swordwrath can defend against early rushes while you build your economy. Don't overcommit to military too early!

🏆 Tournament Mode Strategy

Tournament mode requires a completely different approach than campaign. Here's what works:

Round 1-3 (Early Tournament)

Focus on Swordwrath + Archidon combo. Rush to control the center before your opponent. Upgrade castle to level 2 after your first attack wave.

Round 4-6 (Mid Tournament)

Introduce Speartons as tanks. Use Magikill for crowd control. Save at least 500 gold for emergency defense.

Round 7+ (Late Tournament)

Giants become essential. Use UNDEAD skin on your frontline. Always scout before attacking – know what your opponent is building.

🔥 Advanced Battle Tactics

These techniques separate average players from masters:

  • Micro-management: Pull injured units back and replace them with fresh ones
  • Spell timing: Save Magikill's area stun for when enemies clump up
  • Statue control: Never stop fighting for the central statue – it's worth sacrificing units to keep
  • Bait & punish: Send 1-2 Swordwrath to bait enemy spells, then rush with your main army
  • Economy raiding: Send 3 Archidons behind enemy lines to kill miners
